Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Vizorion uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored in your browser to help websites function, improve performance, or personalize content and ads.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: These help ensure the site functions correctly (e.g., loading pages).
  • Analytics Cookies: Via Google Tag Manager, we track how visitors use our website to improve content and usability.
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your interests.
  • Consent Management: We use CookieYes to manage cookie consent banners and preferences.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage or withdraw your consent at any time by using the cookie banner or adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services like Google may also use cookies over which we have no direct control. You can review their policies here:

  • Google Ads & Cookies Policy
  • CookieYes Privacy Center

5. Updates

We may update this policy as needed. Changes will be reflected on this page.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please visit our Contact page.
